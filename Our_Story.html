Many people today are struggling with keeping what they’re struggling in life or a situation in their life inside of their hearts. Research says that music therapy is one way to heal someone’s burdens inside them.


heartbeat is a website that allows people to share their stories that were kept inside their hearts through music and allows them to support and connect with others that shares the same story as you. This solves the problem by healing their inner self and letting them go.
